⚽ World Cup 2026 — Key Growth Catalyst

Mexico co-hosts the 2026 FIFA World Cup (USA/Canada/Mexico, opening June 11). Operators are aggressively pre-positioning: Stake launched its MX site via partner Uno Capali under SEGOB; Betano announced official WC2026 partnership. iGaming volumes are expected to surge sharply into Q2 2026.

🏛️ Regulatory & Tax Pressure

The 50% IEPS tax (applied to gambling products as high social-burden goods) is accelerating market consolidation around financially robust operators. Smaller operators are exiting; global brands are acquiring SEGOB licenses or entering via local partner model (e.g., Stake via Uno Capali).

🏦 Banking Alternative Demand

SPEI remains the dominant payment rail. However, stricter banking regulation is pushing users toward PayCash (Walmart Express, BAZ SuperApp, Afirme), OXXO Pay, and crypto. CoDi QR payments (Banco de México) appeared in at least one operator cashier (Brazino777).

📱 DepositCheckout Dominance

DepositCheckout operates as the dominant multi-tenant PSP aggregator across the market: 7 of 10 betting operators route SPEI, OXXO, Mercado Pago, or PayCash flows through DepositCheckout subdomains (payin / payment / cashier). etpay serves as the downstream bank-redirect sublayer.

💳 Payment Methods Overview

11 distinct payment method categories identified across the Mexico market audit.

🏦 SPEI — Bank Transfer

Universal. Sistema de Pagos Electrónicos Interbancarios. Instant. Requires a 18-digit CLABE number assigned per transaction. Key fields: CLABE, amount (exact), beneficiary name, bank (STP / STPMEX / SOFOM ENT). Confirmed across all 10 betting + all 6 forex merchants.

🏪 OXXO Pay — Convenience Store

Mexico's largest convenience chain (~20,000 stores). User pays cash with a reference number at OXXO. Clearing time up to 5 minutes. Provider: SafeCharge (Caliente), Pagsmile (Mostbet), DepositCheckout (Roobet, Jugabet, Mostbet-2, Pocket Option).

💵 PayCash — Cash at Retail

Convention-number-based cash payment network. Accepted at Walmart Express, BAZ SuperApp, Afirme, Bodega Aurrera (limited). Confirmed: 1win (conv# 198, payment.depositcheckout.com), Jugabet (pagsmile.com — "Service: PAYCASH, uniform code everywhere").

📲 Mercado Pago — SPEI/CLABE Hybrid

Users fund via Mercado Pago app using a CLABE assigned by the merchant's PSP. Effectively SPEI rails routed through Mercado Pago as banking app. Confirmed: 1xBet (cashier.depositcheckout.com), Jugabet (depositcheckout.com), Melbet (cashier.depositcheckout.com).

🏛️ Bank Redirect — etpay

Select-your-bank flow (BBVA, Banamex, Banorte, Santander) via etpay (qt.etpay.com). Operated as sublayer under DepositCheckout (1xBet: payin.depositcheckout.com → etpay), SmartFastPay (22bet), and Melbet (direct etpay for Banorte).

🎫 Paysafecard — Prepaid Voucher

International prepaid voucher. Caliente: paysafecard.com → safetypay.com chain. Final instructions via SafetyPay. Niche method, primarily for users without bank accounts.

🎫 Todito Cash — Mexican Prepaid

Mexico-specific prepaid card product. Confirmed: Caliente (safecharge.com), Mostbet (depositcheckout.com). Low coverage vs SPEI/OXXO but serves banked + unbanked users.

📷 CoDi — QR / Barcode

Banco de México instant QR payment system. Brazino777: user scans displayed barcode with their banking app. Only one operator confirmed offering CoDi. Growing adoption as banking apps add CoDi support.

💳 Cards (Visa / MC / Amex)

Limited presence due to chargeback risk. 1win: American Express via cardpay.com (Unlimint). RoboForex: cash/card via cardpay.com (Unlimint). Both forex and betting segments show low card adoption; local alternatives dominate.

📦 Retail Cash (Multi-Store)

IQ Option accepts cash at BBVA branches, Bodega Aurrera, 7-Eleven, Farmacias del Ahorro, Scotiabank — via PayRetailers (checkout.payretailers.com) and SafetyPay. Pocket Option: Latam Cash via paywall.payretailers.com → gateway.safetypay.com.

📱 Apple Pay / eWallet

Apple Pay observed at Roobet (standalone option). SticPay eWallet at RoboForex (pay.sticpay.com). Both are niche, indicating that some operators are exploring NFC/wallet options for higher-value or international users.

🗂️ Transaction Field Prioritization

Key data fields observed across SPEI, OXXO, and PayCash flows. Priority defined by payment success risk.

Priority Field Method Description Critical Risk
P1
 CLABE SPEI · Mercado Pago 18-digit interbank account code. Unique per transaction. Bank: STP, STPMEX, or SOFOM ENT. Wrong CLABE = permanent loss of funds
P1
 Monto exacto (Exact Amount) SPEI · Mercado Pago · PayCash Transfer must match the displayed amount exactly. Underpayment or overpayment = transaction unlinked or rejected. Off-by-1-peso = rejected / unlinked
P1
 Número de referencia (Reference Code) OXXO · PayCash · Paysafecard Merchant-assigned reference given at cashier. User reads out at OXXO / Walmart. Must be entered exactly. Wrong reference = payment cannot be attributed
P2
 Número de convenio (Convention Number) PayCash PayCash service number identifying the merchant. Example: 1win = 198. User tells cashier "pago de servicio PayCash, convenio 198". Wrong convenio = payment goes to wrong merchant
P2
 Beneficiario SPEI Beneficiary name displayed in SPEI instructions. Typically user's full name. Must match bank records (some providers require exact match). Name mismatch may trigger bank AML hold
P2
 Banco / Institución SPEI Receiving bank in SPEI transfer. PSPs using STP as intermediary display "STP", "STPMEX", or "SOFOM ENT". etpay bank-redirect flows display full bank names (BBVA, Banamex, Banorte, Santander). Wrong bank = SPEI rejection
P3
 Concepto / Descripción SPEI Payment description field. Not always required for matching, but some PSPs use it for transaction reference linkage. Low risk of failure if omitted. Optional on most platforms
P4
 Código de barras (Barcode) CoDi / QR Barcode/QR code displayed in Brazino777 CoDi flow. User scans with banking app. Fields: implicit in scan data — no manual entry required. Low risk — scan-based, no typo possible
💡 Key Insights & Conclusions

Analytical findings from the May 2026 Mexico cashier audit.

DepositCheckout Aggregator Pattern

DepositCheckout operates as de-facto standard for MX iGaming deposits. 7 of 10 betting operators route through one or more of its subdomains (payin / payment / cashier). Its modular subdomain architecture separates method types: payin for SPEI+bank-redirect, payment for cash networks, cashier for Mercado Pago.

etpay as Universal Bank-Redirect Sublayer

etpay (qt.etpay.com) appears consistently behind multiple PSPs (DepositCheckout, SmartFastPay) for bank-redirect flows. 1xBet BBVA, 1xBet Banamex, Roobet BBVA, 22bet SPEI, Melbet Banorte — all route through etpay. This makes etpay a critical dependency for the MX bank-redirect segment.

H2H SPEI Presence

Multiple operators maintain direct H2H SPEI capability: 1win (SPEI H2H confirmed), Jugabet (SPEI Personal H2H confirmed), Deriv (SPEI H2H via Unlimint), eToro (Bank Transfer H2H, licensed). H2H flows eliminate PSP margin and latency, but require direct banking relationships or licensed infrastructure.

OXXO Dual-Provider Pattern

Some operators offer OXXO Pay through multiple providers simultaneously (Mostbet: Pagsmile + DepositCheckout; Roobet: DepositCheckout; Jugabet: DepositCheckout). This suggests PSP redundancy for high-volume cash channels — if one fails, fallback is available.

PayCash Convention-Number Standardization Risk

Jugabet research note flagged: "1 flow and code everywhere — Service: PAYCASH". If PayCash convention numbers are shared across transactions (not unique per deposit), reconciliation is PSP-side only. The convention number (e.g., 1win = 198) is merchant-level, not transaction-level — reference code alone disambiguates.

Forex vs iGaming PSP Divergence

Clear PSP segmentation: iGaming uses DepositCheckout + etpay + Pagsmile; Forex uses SafetyPay + PayRetailers + CostaPago + Unlimint. Only Cardpay/Unlimint overlaps both (1win Amex + RoboForex cash + Deriv H2H). This reflects different risk tolerance and compliance frameworks per vertical.

Mercado Pago as SPEI-over-App Channel

Mercado Pago deposit flows (1xBet, Jugabet, Melbet) are functionally SPEI transfers using Mercado Pago as the bank app. A CLABE is assigned by DepositCheckout; user makes a SPEI transfer from within Mercado Pago. Not a separate payment method — SPEI rails underneath. Merchant benefit: larger unbanked segment uses MP as their primary banking interface.

CoDi QR — Emerging, Single Operator

Brazino777 is the only audited operator offering CoDi (Banco de México QR system). Given CoDi's growing support in major banking apps (BBVA, Banamex, HSBC), this is likely to expand to more operators in 2026, particularly as World Cup traffic drives mobile-first user acquisition.
